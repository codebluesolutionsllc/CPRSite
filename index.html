Empowering Life-Saving Skills

We provide advanced training for medical professionals and conference speakers to enhance skills and save lives.

About Us

Our mission is to deliver high-quality courses in first aid, advanced trauma life support, and professional development for healthcare providers.

Heartsaver CPR

The Heartsaver CPR AED course trains participants to give CPR and use an automated external defibrillator (AED) safely and effectively, reflecting AHA science and education.

First Aid

The Heartsaver First Aid course teaches participants to recognize and respond to medical emergencies until professional help arrives, ensuring patient safety in the critical first minutes.

Basic Life Support (BLS)

The AHA BLS course prepares healthcare providers to recognize life-threatening emergencies, perform high-quality CPR, deliver appropriate ventilations, and use an AED efficiently.

Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support (ACLS)

ACLS focuses on high-quality CPR, team dynamics, and early recognition of cardiovascular emergencies, giving providers advanced skills to save lives in critical situations.

Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS)

PALS prepares healthcare providers to recognize and intervene in pediatric emergencies, focusing on respiratory distress, shock, and cardiopulmonary arrest with effective teamwork and intervention.
